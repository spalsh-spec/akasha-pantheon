## Notes on tone

- **No exclamation marks** anywhere. Calibrated calm is the brand.
- **No 🔥 / 🚀 / 💯 emojis** in the caption body. ⟁ is the only allowed emoji-glyph.
- **Lowercase** for the body — uppercase ONLY for the hero typographic poster, never for caption sentences. Lowercase in caption + uppercase in poster = the visual signature of every taste-driven indie-builder account on Insta from 2024 onwards.
- **Long-form caption is fine** on IG — the algorithm rewards dwell time. Don't trim for "engagement."
